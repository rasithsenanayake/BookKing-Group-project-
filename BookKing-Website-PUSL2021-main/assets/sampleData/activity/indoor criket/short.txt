Located in Colombo 3 offers not only Futsal but an indoor cricket court as well. If you are a cricket or futsal lover, this indoor sports centre is a must-visit! CR7 Futsal & Indoor Cricket Court also hosts multiple events throughout the year where they provide seasonal packages for you to choose from. They offer some of the cheapest rates from most courts and arena’s which is something good to keep in mind based on what you are looking for. Opening hours: 7.00am to 12.00am
Contact Number: 076 141 0889 .Price range: Approximately Rs. 2500 upwards. Visit their Facebook page @CR7Futsal or Instagram page @cr7futsal.
